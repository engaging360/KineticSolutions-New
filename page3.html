Website as a Service (WaaS)

Traditional business websites are generally classed as a web-based brochure.

Your new business WebApp or WaaS is a super-charged technology embedded application.

‘Website as a Service' is a new concept that allows "non-tech" business owners to easily keep their websites up-to-date instead of making an initial investment and yet being confronted with unforeseen expenses due to updates, repairs and graphical redesign.
